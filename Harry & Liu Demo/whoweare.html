Overview

Harry & Liu Co. Limited, headquartered in Hong Kong with a main office in Shenzhen, China Mainland, leads the forefront of technological innovation, offering comprehensive services that integrate smart technologies, design expertise, procurement strategies, and manufacturing capabilities. With a commitment to delivering cutting-edge solutions, our dynamic team specializes in the development of smart solutions, intricate design, precise procurement, and reliable manufacturing practices. Additionally, our sister concern software company in India expands our global reach and expertise in providing innovative solutions to businesses worldwide.
